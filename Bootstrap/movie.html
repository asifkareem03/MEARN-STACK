Malik

Malik is a 2021 Indian Malayalam-language political thriller film directed, written and edited by Mahesh Narayanan. The film stars Fahadh Faasil, Vinay Forrt, and Nimisha Sajayan in the lead roles, with Joju George, Dileesh Pothan, and Indrans playing supporting roles

Initial release: 3 August 2021

Director: Mahesh Narayanan

Cold Case

Cold Case is a 2021 Indian Malayalam-language crime thriller film directed by Tanu Balak and written by Sreenath V. Nath, that stars Prithviraj Sukumaran,Aditi Balan, Anil Nedumangad in the lead roles, Lakshmi Priyaa Chandramouli, Anand and Rajesh Hebbar in supporting roles.

Initial release: 30 June 2021

Director: Tanu Balak

Nayattu

Nayattu is a 2021 Indian Malayalam-language political thriller film directed and co-produced by Martin Prakkat and written by Shahi Kabir. It stars Kunchako Boban, Joju George and Nimisha Sajayan in the lead roles and Jaffar Idukki, Anil and Hakkim Shajahan in supporting roles.

Initial release: 8 April 2021

Director: Martin Prakkat

Nizhal

Nizhal (transl. Shadow) is a 2021 Indian mystery thriller film directed by Appu N. Bhattathiri and written by S. Sanjeev. It stars Kunchacko Boban and Nayanthara and also features Izin Hash, Rony David, Saiju Kurup and Divya Prabha in supporting roles

Initial release: April 2021

Director: Appu N. Bhattathiri
